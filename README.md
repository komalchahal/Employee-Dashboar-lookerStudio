Employee Analytics Dashboard

Project Overview:

The Employee Analytics Dashboard was developed to analyze workforce metrics such as employee strength, salary distribution, gender composition, and hiring trends. The dashboard provides a consolidated single-page view for HR performance monitoring.
Built using Google Looker Studio.

Problem Statement:

HR teams require a clear and centralized view of employee data to monitor workforce composition, compensation patterns, and attrition levels. This project aims to present key HR metrics in a structured and interactive format.

Tools and Technologies:
Google Looker Studio
Microsoft Excel

Dashboard Design:
 
Approach Single-page layout for consolidated insights
Clear KPI placement at the top
Visual segmentation for salary, workforce, and trend analysis

Key Insights:

The Specialty Products business unit has the highest employee strength among all business units.

North America has the largest workforce concentration, with 634 employees, making it the dominant location.

Hiring activity experienced a sharp decline in 2016, peaked in 2018, and remained relatively stable from 2019 to 2022.

The majority of the Top 10 highest paid employees belong to the Vice President role, indicating significantly higher compensation at senior leadership levels.

Recommendations:
  
Review workforce distribution across regions to ensure balanced employee allocation beyond North America.

Monitor employee strength in high-concentration business units to maintain operational efficiency.

Maintain stable hiring strategies to reduce sharp fluctuations in recruitment trends.

Periodically evaluate executive compensation structures to ensure alignment with organizational performance and internal equity.
